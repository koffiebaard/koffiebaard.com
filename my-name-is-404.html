My name is 404

My first book! It's a fully illustrated children's book, about a robot named 404.

404 is a robot that lives inside the internet. The little robot gets lost quite often.

Regardless 404 still travels the internet, meeting a lot of new friends while doing so. Friends like a weird robot called 500 and a teapot called 418.

Together they will surely find their destination.

Sidenote: I wrote it in two languages; english and dutch.
